1. The Concept

Our Proof of Existence (PoE) service allows you to prove that a specific digital asset existed at a specific point in time without revealing the contents of the file or requiring a centralized third-party notary. By anchoring cryptographic data to the Bitcoin Blockchain, we provide a proof that is mathematically indisputable and globally verifiable.

2. Privacy & The Merkle Tree

We utilize Client-Side Hashing. Your browser generates a SHA-256 hash locally; your actual file is never uploaded. To ensure scalability, your hash is merged with others into a Merkle Tree.

The "Merge" Logic:

The Bitcoin blockchain records a single Merkle Root—a mathematical summary of thousands of hashes. Your .ots file contains the specific "path" required to prove your hash is part of that Root.

3. How to Verify Your Proof

Once you receive your upgraded .ots file via email, you possess an independent proof. You do not need this website to verify it. Follow these steps to see the proof on the blockchain:

  1. Prepare: Ensure you have your Original File and the .ots file we emailed to you.
  2. Visit a Verifier: Go to OpenTimestamps.org or use any local OTS client.
  3. Upload: Drag and drop your .ots file into the verifier.
  4. Match: The verifier will ask for the original file to confirm the hash matches the one inside the proof.
  5. View Result: The tool will display the Bitcoin Block Height and the exact timestamp when the data was anchored. You can then click the block number to see the transaction on a Bitcoin explorer like Mempool.space.

4. Critical Responsibility Notice

Non-Custodial Service:

We do not store your files or your proofs long-term. Our system is designed to facilitate the anchoring and delivery process only. You are the sole custodian of your evidence. You must keep the original file and the .ots file together. If either is lost, the proof becomes mathematically impossible to verify.
